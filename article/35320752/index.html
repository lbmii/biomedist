Structural basis of RNA conformational switching in the transcriptional regulator 7SK RNP

Tuesday, 22 March 2022
Molecular Cell PubMed PMC
Graphical Abstract

Highlights

  • Cryo-EM structures of MePCE-7SK-Larp7 core RNP complex for linear and circular 7SK
  • MePCE is in a methyltransferase-inhibited conformation
  • MePCE interaction with Larp7 is RNA dependent and reveals unexpected motifs
  • 7SK RNA transitions from nascent linear to circular conformation upon core assembly
