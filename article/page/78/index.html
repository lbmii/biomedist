Oridonin attenuates lung inflammation and fibrosis in silicosis via covalent targeting iNOS
Highlights Oridonin alleviates the progression of pulmonary inflammation and fibrosis in silicosis mice. Oridonin inhibits silica-induced alveolar macrophage injury and inflammation. Oridonin covalently targets iNOS and inhibits its level and activity, thereby providing a new potential strategy for the prevention and treatment of silicosis.
