Timing and cell specificity of senescence drives postnatal lung development and injury
Abstract Senescence causes age-related diseases and stress-related injury. Paradoxically, it is also essential for organismal development. Abstract The protective efficacy of serum antibody results from the interplay of antigen-specific B cell clones of different affinities and specificities.
