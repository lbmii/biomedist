Interactomics: dozens of viruses, co-evolving with humans, including the influenza A virus, may actively distort human ageing
Abstract Some viruses (e.g. HIV-1, SARS-CoV-2) have been experimentally proposed to accelerate features of human ageing and of cellular senescence.
