Discovery of Protein-RNA Interactions
catRAPID is an algorithm to estimate the binding propensity of protein-RNA pairs. By combining secondary structure, hydrogen bonding and van der Waals contributions, catRAPID predicts protein-RNA associations with great accuracy.

Explore protein-protein interaction networks with online dababases
STRING is a database of known and predicted protein-protein interactions. The interactions include direct (physical) and indirect (functional) associations; they stem from computational prediction, from knowledge transfer between organisms, and from interactions aggregated from other (primary) databases.
