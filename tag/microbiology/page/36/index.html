The cGAS-STING pathway drives type I IFN immunopathology in COVID-19
Abstract COVID-19, which is caused by infection with SARS-CoV-2, is characterized by lung pathology and extrapulmonary complications. Type I interferons (IFNs) have an essential role in the pathogenesis of COVID-19.
