ACSL4 is essential for radiation-induced intestinal injury by initiating ferroptosis
Abstract Lipid peroxidation-induced ferroptosis is a newly recognized type of programmed cell death. Abstract Ferroptosis is a novel cell death method discovered in recent years. It is usually accompanied by massive accumulations of iron and lipid peroxidation during cell death.
