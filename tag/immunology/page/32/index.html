Abstract Caspases are a family of conserved cysteine proteases that play key roles in programmed cell death and inflammation. Abstract Antiviral immunity in insects is mediated by the RNA interference (RNAi) pathway. Viruses evade antiviral RNAi by expressing virulence factors known as viral suppressors of RNAi (VSR).
