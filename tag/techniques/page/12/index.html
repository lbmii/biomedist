Analysis of extracellular vesicle DNA at the single-vesicle level by nano-flow cytometry
Abstract It has been demonstrated recently that extracellular vesicles (EVs) carry DNA; however, many fundamental features of DNA in EVs (EV-DNA) remain elusive.
